Privacy Policy
Last updated: 15 March 2026

This privacy policy explains how TyreScan (operated by Alpha Pariah Studios, a sole trader based in Wrexham, Wales, UK) collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use this website at tyrescan.app.

We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR).

Who we are

TyreScan is operated by Alpha Pariah Studios (sole trader). You can contact us at: business@alphapariah.studio

What data we collect

We collect the following personal data:

  • Email address — if you voluntarily sign up to be notified about the TyreScan app launch. This is optional.

We do not collect your name, address, payment information, or any other personal details.

The tyre information you enter into the tool (DOT codes, tyre sizes) is processed entirely in your browser and is never sent to our servers.

How we use your data

If you provide your email address, we use it solely to:

  • Send you a single notification when the TyreScan app launches

We will never sell your email address, share it with third parties for marketing, or use it for any other purpose.

Legal basis for processing

We process your email address on the basis of consent (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(a)). You can withdraw your consent at any time by emailing us at business@alphapariah.studio and we will delete your data promptly.

Cookies and tracking

TyreScan uses the following cookies and third-party services that may set cookies on your device:

  • Amazon Associates — affiliate links to Amazon products may set tracking cookies if you click them. Amazon's privacy policy applies: amazon.co.uk privacy notice

We do not use analytics cookies or advertising cookies at this time. If this changes, we will update this policy and display a consent banner.

Third-party affiliate links

This site contains affiliate links to third-party retailers including Amazon, Blackcircles, and Halfords. If you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. These third parties have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

Data retention

We retain email addresses collected for app launch notifications until the app has launched and notification emails have been sent, after which they are deleted. You may request deletion at any time.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights, email us at business@alphapariah.studio.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect any personal data we hold. Email addresses collected are stored securely and accessed only by Alpha Pariah Studios.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect when it was last changed. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
